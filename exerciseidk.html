Kpop Groups Lighsticks

IZ*ONE LIGHSTICK

On March 21, 2019, through a photo teaser on IZ*ONE's social media, the official IZ*ONE Lightstick was officially announced. On March 29, 2019, it was officially released, that same day a video was uploaded to Vlive where the members of IZONE presented their Lightstick, how it looked, how it worked, and what it meant. The lightstick contains 12 small balls, one for each member which also has their corresponding official colour. For a limited time you could buy accessories for the lightstick










LOONA LIGHSTICK

On June 17, 2020, the teaser for LOONA's official light stick was posted online, with further information released the day after. Similarly to other merchandise, the item was produced by 101X. On September 21, it was announced orders for the light stick would reopen. Two additional accessories were added, a light keyring and 11 different mini flags.
